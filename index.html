Instructions

Welcome to TypeRacer! Follow these instructions to take the typing test:

  1. Select the difficulty level from the dropdown menu.
  2. Read the sample text displayed in the box below the dropdown menu.
  3. Start typing the sample text in the textarea provided.
  4. Your time and words per minute (WPM) will be calculated as you type.
  5. Once you finish typing, your results will be displayed on the right side of the screen.
  6. Click the "Retry" button to take the test again.

Good luck and have fun!
